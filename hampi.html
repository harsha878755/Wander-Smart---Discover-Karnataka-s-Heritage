Hampi was once the capital of the mighty Vijayanagara Empire. Today, it is filled with ancient temples, monuments, and beautiful ruins that are over 600 years old. Every structure and stone here has a story to tell.

Surrounded by huge rocky hills, Hampi feels like a magical land. Each boulder, each path, and each ruin carries memories from centuries ago. The sunrises here make the entire landscape glow with history.

In its golden age, Hampi was a lively and powerful city. The streets were full of markets, music from temples, and travellers from different parts of the world. The Tungabhadra River quietly flowed beside the city and watched everything change with time.

Even though the empire fell long ago, the spirit of Hampi still remains alive.

When you walk through the ruins today, you can feel the calmness, the stories, and the hidden magic of this ancient city.

The Virupaksha Temple still continues its traditions after hundreds of years and remains one of the most important temples in Hampi.

The famous Stone Chariot at the Vittala Temple looks as if it could start moving at any time. It is one of the most iconic monuments of India.

The beautifully carved stepwells and sculptures show the incredible skill and creativity of the people who once lived here.
